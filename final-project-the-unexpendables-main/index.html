About our Firm


Victor and Associates Legal was formed in 2014 by Victor Olamide (SAN, FCIArb, FBR) with the sole mission of providing legal advice and help to those in need. We offer legal advice and assistance to several areas including Property Law, Criminal Defense, Family Law, Intellectual property law, litigation and dispute resolution, banking and finance laws and maritime law.
